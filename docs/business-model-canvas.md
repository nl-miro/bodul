## Overview

Business Model Canvas for Bodul - a price comparison and price history tracking platform for Croatian and Slovenian shoppers, focused on accurate product matching and transparent pricing.

---

## Differentiators

What makes us different?

- **High-quality catalog** - No "uncategorized offers"; consistent taxonomy and attributes
- **Accurate matching** - Product-centric model (e.g., iPhone 17 Pro with variations) to reduce duplicates and wrong comparisons
- **Alert-first roadmap** - Price drop alerts and upcoming sale alerts (post-MVP) to keep users engaged

---

## 1. Customer Segments

Who are we creating value for?

- **Price-conscious consumers** - Individuals looking for the best deals on electronics (Primary)
- **Deal/discount hunters** - Users monitoring prices for upcoming purchases and sales events (Primary)
- **Planners** - People researching purchases before buying (Secondary)
- **Small businesses** - SMBs comparing supplier prices for equipment (Future)

---

## 2. Value Propositions

What value do we deliver to customers?

- **Price comparison (MVP)** - Compare prices across Croatian and Slovenian retailers
- **Price history (MVP)** - See historical trends and price drops
- **Fake sale detection (MVP)** - Spot misleading discounts using price history
- **Time savings (MVP)** - One place to check multiple retailers
- **Availability visibility (MVP)** - Show in-stock status where possible
- **Price drop alerts (Post-MVP)** - Notify when a product hits a target price
- **Upcoming sale alerts (Post-MVP)** - Notify about scheduled/promised promotions
- **Specs comparison (Post-MVP)** - Side-by-side attribute comparison for similar products

---

## 3. Channels

How do we reach our customers?

- **Web application** - Primary platform for price comparison (Planned)
- **Mobile app** - On-the-go price checking (Planned)
- **Email notifications** - Price alerts and deal digests (Future)
- **Social media** - Price alerts and deal notifications (Future)
- **Browser extension** - Price comparisons while browsing retailer sites (Future)

---

## 4. Customer Relationships

What type of relationship do customers expect?

- **Self-service** - Users independently browse and compare prices
- **Automated alerts** - System-generated price drop notifications (Future)
- **Community features** - User reviews and deal sharing (Future)
- **Lightweight support** - Report wrong match / wrong spec / missing offer (Post-MVP)

---

## 5. Revenue Streams

How do we make money?

- **Donations** - Tip jar for users who find value in the service (Near-term)
- **Premium subscriptions** - Advanced features, unlimited alerts, API access (Future)
- **Advertising** - Promoted listings for specific products or sales (Future)

---

## 6. Key Resources

What do we need to deliver our value proposition?

- **Data collection infrastructure** - Scrapers and crawlers for retailer sites
- **Product database** - Normalized product catalog with price history
- **Matching algorithms** - Product identification across retailers
- **Technical team** - Development and maintenance
- **Fast & clean UI** - Fastest possible, well-designed site so users choose us for ease of use

---

## 7. Key Activities

What do we do to deliver value?

- **Data collection** - Scraping retailer catalogs and product pages
- **Data normalization** - Standardizing product information
- **Product matching** - Identifying same products across retailers
- **Platform development** - Building and maintaining web/mobile apps
- **Retailer monitoring** - Tracking retailer sites for data collection

---

## 8. Key Partnerships

Who helps us deliver value?

- **Retailers** - Data source

---

## 9. Cost Structure

What are our major costs?

- **Infrastructure** - Hosting, databases, storage (Variable)
- **Development** - Engineering team (Fixed)
- **Data collection** - Proxy services, compute for scraping (Variable)
- **Marketing** - User acquisition campaigns (Variable)
- **Legal** - Terms of service compliance, potential retailer disputes (Variable)

---

## Notes

- Initial focus: Electronics retailers in Croatia and Slovenia
- MVP priority: Price comparison and history tracking
- Monetization: Donations (Near-term), subscriptions and advertising (Future)
